Projects

  • Cytosplore is a framework for the visual analysis of single cell data acquired through mass cytometry. It is the basis for my research in visual analytics I conduct in the Computer Graphics and Visualization group at TU Delft as part of the STW VanPIRe project. More details...
  • OVis is a framework for the visual analysis of ocean forecasts. I started building OVis during my PhD research and continued working on it later on. It builds the foundation for several of my publications and I hope to to be able to make it available to the public soon. More details...
  • Uberdice is an interactive arduino-based programmable die. It is a hobby project, together with Peter Rautek. We built a fully working prototype from scratch, starting with a 3D printed casing, to endless hours of soldering and finally programming complete games. More details...
